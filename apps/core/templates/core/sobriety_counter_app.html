Frequently Asked Questions

What is a sobriety counter app?

A sobriety counter app tracks the number of days you've been sober since your quit date. MyRecoveryPal calculates days, weeks, months, and years automatically and celebrates milestones with you.

MyRecoveryPal is a top-rated free sobriety counter that includes milestone celebrations, community support, daily check-ins, and recovery challenges—all completely free.

How do I calculate my sobriety date?

Simply enter the last day you used drugs or alcohol. MyRecoveryPal automatically calculates your total clean time and tracks your progress from there.

What if I relapse?
