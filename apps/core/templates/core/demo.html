Share Your Journey

Express yourself through personal posts, journal entries, and updates. Share your struggles, victories, and everyday moments with a community that truly understands.

  • Post updates about your recovery journey
  • Share photos and milestones
  • Choose who sees your posts (public, friends, or private)
  • Get support through likes and comments

Build Real Connections

Follow others who inspire you, make friends, and build a network of support. Connect with people in recovery, active addiction, and loved ones who understand your experience.

  • Follow members and see their updates in your feed
  • Build friendships with people who understand
  • Send private messages for one-on-one support
  • Join groups based on shared interests

Celebrate Every Victory

Track your progress and share your milestones with friends who celebrate every step forward. Whether it's one day or one year, every milestone matters.

  • Track your sobriety days
  • Share milestone achievements
  • Receive encouragement from the community
  • Celebrate others' victories

A Safe Space for Everyone

Whether you're in recovery, actively struggling, or supporting a loved one, you're welcome here. Our community guidelines ensure a respectful, judgment-free environment.

  • Moderated community for safety
  • Report inappropriate content
  • Block users if needed
  • Control your privacy settings

Participate in Challenges & Groups

Join recovery challenges to stay accountable, or find groups that match your interests and recovery path. From meditation to fitness to specific programs, there's a place for everyone.

  • Join 30-day, 60-day, or 90-day challenges
  • Daily check-ins with accountability partners
  • Create or join interest-based groups
  • Share resources within your groups

Stay Connected Anywhere
