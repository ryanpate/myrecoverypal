Frequently Asked Questions

How can I support someone in recovery from a distance?

Your loved one shares their progress — milestones, check-in consistency, mood trends — and you send one-tap encouragement. You stay connected without having to ask "how are you?" every day. They choose what you see and can pause anytime.

Can I track my loved one's sobriety?

Only with their consent. They invite you or approve your request and pick a sharing level — from milestones only, up to check-in trends and a gentle inactivity alert. Their journal and private notes are never shared.

What if they don't want to share everything?

That's the whole point. The person in recovery controls the level per supporter and can change it or stop sharing at any time. Nothing is shared without their explicit consent.

How much does it cost?

A Supporter membership is $7.99/month. Sharing is always free for the person in recovery — only the supporter subscribes. Cancel anytime.

Is it private and safe?

Yes. Sharing is consent-based and fully controlled by your loved one. Journal entries, cravings, and private notes are never shared. Crisis resources (988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) are always shown.
