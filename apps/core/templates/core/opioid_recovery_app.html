Why MyRecoveryPal Works for Opioid Recovery

Opioid addiction is one of the most challenging addictions to overcome. The physical withdrawal, the powerful cravings, the risk of relapse—it requires strong support. MyRecoveryPal provides that support 24/7.

Understanding Opioid Addiction

Whether you're struggling with heroin, fentanyl, oxycodone, hydrocodone, or other opioids, you're facing a powerful adversary. Opioid addiction literally changes your brain—but recovery is possible, and many have done it.

Features for Opioid Recovery

  • Clean Time Tracking: Track your opioid-free days with milestones at 24 hours, 72 hours (past acute withdrawal), 1 week, 30 days, 90 days, and beyond.
  • Craving Tracking: Log cravings when they hit. Identify triggers and patterns over time.
  • Peer Support: Connect with others who understand the specific hell of opioid withdrawal and recovery.
  • Recovery Groups: Join groups specifically for opioid recovery, or broader addiction groups.
  • Accountability Partners: Get matched with a Recovery Pal for one-on-one support.
  • Crisis Resources: Quick access to SAMHSA helpline, overdose prevention, and Narcan information.
