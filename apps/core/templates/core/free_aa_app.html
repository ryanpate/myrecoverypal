How MyRecoveryPal Supports Your AA Program

MyRecoveryPal isn't meant to replace AA meetings, sponsors, or the fellowship. It's designed to complement your program by providing support between meetings and helping you stay connected to recovery 24/7.

Filling the Gaps Between Meetings

AA meetings are powerful—but they typically happen a few times per week. What about the other times when cravings hit, when you're feeling low, or when you just need to connect with someone who understands? That's where MyRecoveryPal comes in.

Features That Complement AA

  • Sobriety Tracking: Track your clean time with milestone celebrations that mirror AA chips (24 hours, 30 days, 90 days, 1 year, etc.).
  • Daily Check-ins: Practice Step 10 with daily self-reflection on mood, cravings, and gratitude.
  • Recovery Community: Connect with others working the steps. Share experience, strength, and hope—just like in meetings.
  • Private Journal: Work through Step 4 writing, daily inventory, or personal reflections in a private space.
  • Accountability Partners: Connect with a Recovery Pal for additional support between sponsor calls.
  • Recovery Groups: Join groups focused on specific steps, recovery stages, or shared interests.

Not Officially Affiliated with AA

MyRecoveryPal is an independent platform designed to support people in 12 step programs. We're not officially affiliated with Alcoholics Anonymous, but many of our members are active in AA and find the app helpful alongside their program.

AA doesn't have an official app, but MyRecoveryPal is designed to complement your AA program. Track sobriety, connect with others in recovery, and get support between meetings. Free to join with optional Premium.

What is the best free app for AA recovery?

MyRecoveryPal is a free-to-join app for people in AA. It offers sobriety tracking, peer support, recovery groups, challenges, and community features—all designed to support your 12 step journey. Core features are free, with optional Premium for advanced tools.

MyRecoveryPal is free to join. Core features including the social feed, groups, challenges, check-ins, and journaling are free. We also offer Premium ($4.99/month) with advanced features like the AI Recovery Coach, detailed analytics, and unlimited groups.

Absolutely. Post anonymously, control your profile privacy, and share only what you're comfortable with. Anonymity is core to recovery, and we respect that tradition.
