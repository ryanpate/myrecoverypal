Authentic Self

Why MyRecoveryPal Exists

MyRecoveryPal was created with a simple but powerful vision: to help people in addiction recovery connect with each other in meaningful ways. We believe that recovery happens best in community, where people can share their experience, strength, and hope with others who truly understand the journey.

This isn't just another health platform or resource directory. MyRecoveryPal is a social network specifically designed for the recovery community—a place where people in recovery, those considering it, those in active addiction, and their loved ones can all come together to support each other.

We're working to destigmatize addiction by creating a space where people can be their authentic selves without shame or judgment. Here, vulnerability is strength, honesty is celebrated, and every day of recovery—whether it's day one or day one thousand—matters.

Our goal is simple: to make consistent, long-term recovery more possible by facilitating real connections between people who care about recovery. Because when we recover together, we recover stronger.

What We Believe

Connection Over Isolation

Recovery thrives in community. We're building a social network where real connections help people stay strong in their recovery journey.

Breaking the Stigma

Addiction is not a moral failing. We're creating a judgment-free space where people can be open about their recovery without shame.

Family & Friends Matter

Recovery affects everyone. We welcome family members and friends who want to understand and support their loved ones.

Authentic Living

We help people in recovery gain the confidence to be their true selves and live authentically in their new sober life.
