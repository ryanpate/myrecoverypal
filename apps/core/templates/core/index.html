A Free Online Recovery Community for Everyone

MyRecoveryPal is a free online community designed for individuals seeking addiction recovery support, sobriety help, and mental health resources. Whether you're in active recovery from alcohol or drugs, supporting a loved one struggling with addiction, or looking for alternatives to traditional 12-step programs, you'll find a welcoming space here.

What Makes MyRecoveryPal Different

Unlike other recovery apps or addiction forums, MyRecoveryPal combines the best features of social media with recovery-focused tools. Our platform offers sobriety tracking, milestone celebrations, private journaling, and real-time connection with others who understand your journey. It's completely free—no subscriptions, no hidden fees, no paywalls.

Who MyRecoveryPal Serves

  • People in recovery from alcohol, drugs, or behavioral addictions
  • Individuals in active addiction seeking support and hope
  • Family members and friends supporting loved ones through recovery
  • Anyone seeking mental health support and peer connection
  • Those looking for 12-step alternatives or complementary support

Recovery Support Features

Track your days sober, celebrate recovery milestones with your community, share your story through posts and photos, join recovery challenges, participate in supportive groups, and build genuine friendships with others who understand. Our mobile-friendly platform ensures your support network is always accessible, whether you're at home or on the go.
