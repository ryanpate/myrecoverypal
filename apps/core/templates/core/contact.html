Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

Is MyRecoveryPal free?

Yes, MyRecoveryPal is completely free. We believe financial circumstances should never be a barrier to recovery support. All features are available to all users at no cost.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from your Settings page. Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Is my information private?

Your privacy is our top priority. Journal entries are always private. You control what information is visible on your profile. We never sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use MyRecoveryPal anonymously?

Yes. You can use a nickname, keep your profile private, and post anonymously in groups. Many users choose not to use their real names.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the three-dot menu on any post and select "Report." Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email us directly for urgent concerns.

About MyRecoveryPal

MyRecoveryPal is a social recovery platform built to help people in addiction and mental health recovery connect, support each other, and stay accountable. We're a small team passionate about making recovery support accessible to everyone.

Whether you're on day 1 or year 10, there's a place for you here. Join our community of people who understand the journey and are here to support each other.
