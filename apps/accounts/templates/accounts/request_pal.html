What is a Recovery Pal?

A recovery pal is a peer who:

  • Provides mutual support and accountability
  • Shares similar recovery experiences
  • Offers non-judgmental listening
  • Celebrates wins and helps through challenges
  • Maintains regular check-ins
Making a Good Request
  • Be genuine about your recovery journey
  • Explain what you can offer in return
  • Set realistic expectations
  • Be specific about your goals
  • Respect their decision
Pal Best Practices
  • Maintain regular communication
  • Be honest and supportive
  • Respect boundaries
  • Share both struggles and successes
  • Keep confidentiality
Remember

A recovery pal is a peer support relationship, not professional therapy or medical advice. Always consult healthcare providers for medical concerns.
