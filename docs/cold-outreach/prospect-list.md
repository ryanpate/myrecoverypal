# Outreach prospect list — recovery facilities within ~100 mi of Springfield, IL

Compiled June 2026 from SAMHSA findtreatment.gov, Illinois DHS/SUPR, official
facility sites, and sober-living directories. **Verify each contact before
sending** — details drift, and most facilities don't publish a direct email.

## ⚠️ The email reality (read first)

Of every facility below, **only Centerstone (Alton) publishes a usable email
address.** Treatment centers almost universally route contact through a web
form or phone, not a public inbox. So for cold *email* you have two real paths:

1. **Call the main line** and ask for the best email for the **DUI / court-services
   coordinator** or **admissions / clinical director** — that's your buyer.
2. **Use the site contact form** (works, but lower response than a named inbox).

Many of these are **multi-site organizations** (Gateway, Rosecrance, Chestnut,
Centerstone). One well-placed contact can cover several locations — efficient,
but also means a corporate gatekeeper. Independent sober-living homes are the
opposite: no central office, contact house-by-house.

**Confidence key:** ✅ = I verified name/address/phone (and email) directly on
the official site this session. 🔹 = from a primary/official source via research
but not re-verified — confirm before sending. ❓ = lead worth checking; details
unconfirmed.

many have solicitation policies — keep it brief, factual, and offer the public
verification link as the hook (no sales pitch). Lower priority than facilities.
