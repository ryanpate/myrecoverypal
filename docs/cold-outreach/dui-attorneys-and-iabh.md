## ⚠️ Email reality (same lesson as the facilities)

I checked each firm's site for a published inbox. **None of these firms publish a
direct email** — they all use a contact form (several run the same Justia-built
template, including an identical "contact form sends information by non-encrypted
email" disclaimer). So for attorneys, the realistic paths are:

1. **The site contact form** (fastest, works now).
2. **A quick call** to get the attorney's or paralegal's direct email.
3. **Avvo / Justia / LinkedIn profiles** — individual attorneys sometimes list a
   direct email there even when the firm site doesn't.

The audience is still worth it: every DUI client has a court/Secretary-of-State
compliance obligation, and attorneys like tools that keep clients out of
violation. Pitch the **court-liaison variant** reframed as a *referral aid*.

> ⚠️ Three firms (Vig, Harvatin, Hanken) returned the **same address, 1100 S 5th
> St** — likely a site-template artifact. Phones and websites are reliable;
> **confirm each street address** before using.

## IABH — Illinois Association for Behavioral Health

Statewide trade association for addiction/mental-health prevention, treatment, and
recovery providers. A listing/sponsorship reaches **every member treatment org at
once** — far more efficient than cold-emailing each facility.

- **Website:** ilabh.org
- **Email:** info@ilabh.org ✅ (real, published)
- **Phone:** (217) 528-7335
- **Address:** 937 South Second St, Springfield, IL 62704 (local to you)

### Which membership fits MyRecoveryPal
You're a **vendor doing business with providers**, so the right tier is
**Corporate Membership** — *not* Agency (that's for treatment providers) and
*not* Individual (ICB-credentialed clinicians). Email info@ilabh.org and ask
specifically about:
- **Corporate membership** cost and benefits
- **Conference sponsorship / exhibitor table** (their annual conference puts you
  in front of decision-makers from member agencies in one room)
- **Newsletter / member-communication advertising** options

### Member benefits referenced (context on the org's value)
National Council for Mental Wellbeing membership ($5,087 value), advocacy +
legislative updates, conference registration discounts, members-only resources,
complimentary Capitol Fax subscription ($500 value). Agency dues are a sliding
scale based on Illinois behavioral-health budget; corporate pricing on request.

> **Best move:** one email to **info@ilabh.org** asking about corporate
> membership + conference sponsorship/advertising. That single contact is likely
> higher-ROI than the entire facility cold-email list — it's the one published,
> staffed inbox that fronts the whole provider network, and it's based in
> Springfield.
