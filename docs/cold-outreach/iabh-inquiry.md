# IABH partnership inquiry email

One-to-one inquiry to the Illinois Association for Behavioral Health about
corporate membership, conference sponsorship, and newsletter advertising.

**Send as plain text from `ryan@myrecoverypal.com`** — this is a personal business
inquiry to a staffed inbox, not bulk outreach, so it doesn't need the CAN-SPAM
footer or unsubscribe link the facility cold-emails use.

- **To:** info@ilabh.org
- **Subject:** Springfield-based recovery platform — corporate membership & sponsorship inquiry

---

Hi,

I'm Ryan Pate, founder of MyRecoveryPal, a recovery-support platform based here in
Springfield. I'm reaching out to learn the best way for a vendor like us to engage
with IABH and its member agencies.

We've built a tool I think your members would find genuinely useful for their
court-involved clients. It lets people in recovery log their meeting attendance —
program-neutral across AA, NA, SMART, Refuge Recovery, and secular options — and
generate a court-ready PDF report with a tamper-evident verification code. For
agencies serving DUI, drug-court, and other court-ordered clients, it takes the
attendance-verification and paperwork burden off clinical staff while giving
clients documentation that holds up with courts and probation officers.

Rather than reach out to member agencies one by one, I'd much rather do this the
right way through IABH. Could you point me to information on:

- Corporate membership — cost and benefits for a vendor partner
- Conference sponsorship / exhibitor opportunities at your annual conference
- Newsletter or member-communication advertising options

Being local to Springfield, I'd also welcome the chance to stop by or talk by
phone if that's easier.

Thank you for the work IABH does for behavioral health across Illinois.

Best,
Ryan Pate
Founder, MyRecoveryPal
ryan@myrecoverypal.com
www.myrecoverypal.com

---

## Why it's written this way
- Leads with **value to their members**, then the ask — associations field vendor
  pitches constantly; "help me do this the right way through you" lands better
  than a hard sell.
- Uses the **Springfield-local** angle as a soft offer to meet.
- Deliberately omits pricing/sales detail — this email's only job is to open the
  door and get their corporate-partner info.

See `dui-attorneys-and-iabh.md` for IABH contact details and membership context.
