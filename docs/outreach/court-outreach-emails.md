# Court Compliance — Referral-Source Outreach Emails

**Audience:** probation officers, drug court coordinators, DUI program
administrators, public defenders. These are the distribution wedge for the
Court Compliance tier (roadmap Phase 3).

**Rules for sending:**
- Send individually, personalized — never BCC-blast. One bad impression with
  a county office travels.
- No attachments on the first email (spam filters + gov IT). Link to
  https://www.myrecoverypal.com/for-probation-officers/ instead; offer a
  sample PDF on reply.
- The ask is *feedback*, not a sale. "Would you accept this?" converts
  better than "buy this," and their answer genuinely shapes the product.
- The PO never pays. The defendant subscribes ($29.99/mo). Say this early —
  it defuses the "what's this going to cost my department" reflex.
- Sign with full name + founder title + phone if comfortable. Gov employees
  respond better to a person than a brand.
- Follow up once after ~7 days. Stop after two touches.

## Tracking

Log each send in a simple sheet: date, name, office, email variant,
response (none / no / interested / objection). Objections are product
input — feed them back into the court feature roadmap (chair signatures and
QR check-in are the most likely asks; both are already on the Phase 2
deferred list).
